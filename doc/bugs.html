• The current date routines limit the dates to the years: 1970 - 2037

  • The current date routines assume earth time, so they will not work for SF stories set on planets with different day/month/year cycles.

    • If set in space, and earth seconds are used as a constant, then a 25 hour day could be a rational length for days, which would be exactly 90,000 sec long. Weeks could be 10 days long, grouped into 4 week months, then 10 month years, which works out to 10,000 hours/year.

    24 * 365 = 8760 hours/year
25 * 50 * 10 = 12500 hours/year

10,000 hours/year / 25 days = 400
400 / 10 months = 40 days/month
40 days/month / 10 days/week = 4 weeks
